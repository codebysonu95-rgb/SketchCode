Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

At SketchCode, your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we ensure its protection while providing you with high-quality source codes and developer tools.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and Email address provided via Google Sign-in to personalize your experience.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and non-personal identifiers used strictly for analytics and security purposes.
  • Usage Data: Interaction logs to help us improve app performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the SketchCode Store.
  • To personalize and expand our platform's features based on user needs.
  • To enhance security and monitor for fraudulent activities or unauthorized access.
  • To process your transactions securely via Razorpay.
  • For app maintenance, analytics, and security monitoring.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information is shared only with trusted third-party services strictly required to operate the app, such as payment processing and authentication.

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. While we use industry-standard security measures, please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes the following services to ensure smooth operation:

  • Google Play Services (Authentication)
  • Firebase (Real-time Database & Storage)
  • Razorpay (Secure Payment Gateway)

5. User Control & Data Deletion

We believe in giving users full control over their data:

  • Users may request account or data deletion at any time.
  • To request deletion, please contact us via our official email provided below.
  • Requests will be processed in accordance with applicable laws and platform policies.

6. Children's Privacy

SketchCode does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our app, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately to remove such data.
