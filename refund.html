Refund & Cancellation

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Thank you for choosing SketchCode. As we deal in digital assets, we have established the following policy to ensure a fair transaction process for both publishers and buyers.

1. Digital Products Policy

Since SketchCode offers non-tangible, irrevocable digital goods, we do not issue refunds once the order is confirmed and access to the digital product has been granted. By completing a purchase, you acknowledge that the digital nature of the product prevents any return or reversal of the delivery.

2. Exceptional Circumstances

Refund requests are only considered under the following specific conditions:

  • Double Payment: Confirmed multiple charges for the same item due to a technical error.
  • Critical Defects: If the source code is fundamentally broken or does not match the description, and our team cannot resolve it within 7 days.
  • Clarification: Minor bugs, version incompatibilities (Sketchware Pro versions), or user-specific environment issues do not qualify as major defects.

3. Regional Availability & Credits

Currently, credit purchases and withdrawals are supported only for users located in India.

  • International payments and withdrawals are not available at this time.
  • Any attempt to bypass regional restrictions via VPN or other methods may lead to account suspension.
  • International support may be introduced in the future at our sole discretion.

4. Cancellation Policy

Due to the instantaneous delivery of digital content and project files, orders cannot be cancelled once the transaction is processed through the payment gateway.

⚠️ Final Decision Right: SketchCode reserves the final and discretionary right to approve or reject any refund request based on internal verification, access logs, and policy compliance.

5. Refund Process

Requests must be submitted via email within 48 hours of the transaction. Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment source within 5-7 business days via Razorpay.
