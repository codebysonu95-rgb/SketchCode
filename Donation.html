Donation & Support

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

SketchCode is now a 100% Free Platform for all developers. We have removed all in-app purchase systems and credits to provide open access to high-quality source codes and projects.

1. Free Content Policy

All digital assets, source codes, and projects available on SketchCode are completely free to download and use for educational purposes. SketchCode does not charge users for any content hosted on our platform.

2. Developer Support

Users who wish to show appreciation to developers may do so via external support links provided by the developers (e.g., Buy Me a Coffee, UPI, GitHub Sponsors, etc.).

  • These links are added by individual developers at their own discretion.
  • SketchCode does not facilitate, process, or monitor these external transactions.
  • Users are encouraged to verify the developer before sending any voluntary support.

3. No Liability

SketchCode (CodeBySonu) is not responsible for any transactions, disputes, or issues that arise through external support links.

  • Since these transactions happen outside our platform, we cannot issue refunds or intervene in payment disputes.
  • Any support or donation sent to a developer is a direct voluntary transaction between you and the developer.

⚠️ Non-Refundable: Any voluntary support or donation sent to a developer through external links is considered a final gift and is generally non-refundable. Please contact the respective developer directly for any inquiries regarding your support.
